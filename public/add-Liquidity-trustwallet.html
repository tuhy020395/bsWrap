How to Add Liquidity on TrustWallet

    Step 1

    Go to the exchange page here.

    Step 2

    Unlock your Binance Smart Chain Wallet (top right-hand side or below).

    Step 3

    You click on : “WalletConnect”

    Step 4

    Then a QR code will appear on the screen.

    Step 5

    Open the Trustwallet app on your mobile device. Go to settings, and click on "WalletConnect" to scan the QR code.

    Step 6

    A notification will be sent to your phone
    Click on "Submit" to complete the wallet connection.

    Step 7

    Now we will add Liquidity
    You can choose to switch to the Liquidity section

    Step 8

    You will click on “Add liquidity”
    You can choose to switch to the Liquidity section

    Step 9

    Click on “Select a currency”

    Step 10

    Enter "BSCS" to select the BSCS token

    Step 11

    Enter the BNB amount, then press Supply
    Note: You need to have some amount of BSCS and BNB before that in order to add liqidity

    Step 12

    A confirmation screen will appear, choose “Confirm Supply”

    Wait a moment for the system to validate, and you successfully added the liquidity
