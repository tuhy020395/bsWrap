Frequently Asked Questions?

BSCS Frequently Asked Questions?

Question 1: Why BSC Station?

We believe that communities are important contributors to solving the problems that remain in the digital asset market. BSCS believes that with a quality ecosystem, determined by the community, a transparent and optimal operating model will be the best destination for users.

BSCS provides the ability to issue new assets Pool at the lowest cost and create highly liquid capital pools through our platform. There are no restrictions on the initial price nor the ratio of funds and tokens supplied.

BSCS is both a Dex and Project startor; It is the incubator for the development of new projects.

The BSCS token economics model introduces a membership system and a fee sharing mechanism. In addition to its governance functions, BSCS gives holders trading fee discounts and IDO participation allocations and token Hodler airdrop. The system’s entitle holders to trading fee dividends and membership rewards, as well as the BSCS holder benefits.

Question 4: How do I swap with BSCD?

You can start swapping on BSCD by accessing the bscstation.finance page and choose the tokens, along with the amount that you would like to swap. For detailed instructions, you can check out our docs.

  • BSC Swap page: bscstation.finance

Question 5: Why are the group chats of BSCS having so many poor – English users?

As mentioned earlier, the team of BSCS consists of people from all different parts of the world, the members of BSCS communities also are from different parts of the globe. Therefore, to the members not from English-speaking countries, communication is not always the easiest task. Despite that, we wanted to keep our groups and communities this way as creating a healthy economic Ecosystem for everyone regardless of their nationality, race or color has always been our ultimate purpose. No matter where you are from, once coming to BSCS community, you shall be well informed and supported as a friend. A sustainable and healthy economy is for everyone and takes everyone joining hands to create. Come to us and BSCS Ecosystem and together, we can create a brighter future.
