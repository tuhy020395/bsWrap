BSCStation Tokenomics (BSCS - BEP20)

BSCS Token (BSCS - BEP20)

BSC Station has only raised the most necessary funds for Product Development and to help the BSCS ecosystem grow sustainably, bringing more value to users through our mainstream products.

All allocation for Team member, Advisors, Partner and Foundation tokens are locked for at least 1 year, the rest of the tokens are allocated to the community via Token sale, Bounty and Airdrop, Liquidity Provision and rewards as well as Marketing activities.

Token Specification (BSCS - BEP20)

  • Name: BSCS token
  • Symbol: BSCS
  • Total supply: 500,000,000 BSCS
  • Standard: BEP-20
  • Precision: 18
  • Network: Binance Smart Chain
  • Smart Contract Address: 0xbcb24afb019be7e93ea9c43b7e22bb55d5b7f45d

Token Allocation (BSCS - BEP20)

  • Seed sale (12%): 60,000,000 BSCS

BSC Station Holder Benefits

    The benefits of BSCS Hodler? Here is a quick overview.

    • GET FREE TOKEN BSC Swap - Hodlers holding BSCS token will receive another free token
    • UNLOCK NFT AUCTION - BSCS tokens will be used to pay as Auction fee, transaction, and also for NFT access behind paywall.
    • VOTING - Hodlers holding BSCS or more can vote to approve or reject projects on BSCS IDO
    • SHARING TRANSACTION FEE - Hodlers holding BSCS will share their transaction fees
    • IDO DISCOUNT - Those who own BSCS will automatically qualify for a 10% discount on the IDO sale price.
    • PAYMENT - Use BSCS as a payment method at the service points within BSCS ecosystem in the near future.
    • FUTURE BENEFITS - There are many other benefits of BSCS Hodler that we are building and researching for early implementation.
