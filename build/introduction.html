Introduction

Introduction

BSC Station (BSCS) aims to build a Full-Stack DEFI with NFT Auction on the Binance Smart Chain. It will become economy infrastructure for DeFi and NFT powered by Binance Smart Chain. The Infrastructure for DeFi & NFT Powered by BSC.

BSCS operates on top of existing blockchains Binance Smart Chain that is designed to offer maximum value to consumers and institutions. Binance Smart Chain has solved the problem of infrastructure in the context of BSCS’s product, and that BSCS represents the ideal use case to leverage the technologies offered by its platform.

Vision

To become a leading ecosystem that provides DeFi services and utilities for crypto users on the Binance Smart Chain platform, develop and optimize applications on BSC and become a leading Defi ecosystem, transparent, user-friendly and bring the most benefits to the user community.

Mission

BSC Station is a community ecosystem, helping users to access DeFi platforms in the easiest, safest and most cost-effective way. BSC Station is the most convenient bridge to connect users and application products on the Binance Smart Chain ecosystem and other Blockchain protocols.

The BSC Station platform uses the Sharing Economy Model for the purpose of profit sharing, providing better liquidity farming and liquidity services to crypto traders and farmer. Depositors provide market liquidity to earn passive income in the form of our BSCS tokens.

The BSCS token is the operating fuel and the governance token of the BSC Station ecosystem. BSCS holders can participate in liquidity farming, share transaction fees on BSC Swap, BSCS Launchpool and many other profit-sharing activities on the BSC Station ecosystem to maximize profits.

Why BSC Station

Why BSC Station

We believe that communities are important contributors to solving the problems that remain in the digital asset market. BSCS believes that with a quality ecosystem, determined by the community, a transparent and optimal operating model will be the best destination for users.

BSCS provides the ability to issue new assets Pool at the lowest cost and create highly liquid capital pools through our platform. There are no restrictions on the initial price nor the ratio of funds and tokens supplied.

BSCS is both a Dex and an Project startor; It is the incubator for the development of new projects

The BSCS token economics model introduces a membership system and a fee sharing mechanism. In addition to its governance functions, BSCS gives holders trading fee discounts and IDO participation allocations and token Hodler airdrop. The system’s entitle holders to trading fee dividends and membership rewards, as well as the BSCS holder benefits.

Why was Binance Smart Chain (BSC) chosen?

The majority of dApps are running on the ERC20 platform with high conversion fees and other fees very expensive. Besides, the block creation also takes a lot of time.

Binance Smart Chain is considered to be the ultimate solution. First, EVM compatibility allows support for Ethereum tools, with faster speeds and cheaper transaction fees. Second, thanks to EVM interoperability and programming, Binance Smart Chain can run on Proof of Stake, thereby increasing decentralization and community engagement. Furthermore, Binance Smart Chain is expected to combine the features of the DEX blockchain with smart contract functionality. From there, it helps developers to program and additionally interact on the blockchain. BSC can fully work even when Binance Chain is offline (Because this is not Layer 2 Blockchain, but independent Blockchain)

Finally, BSC has a huge ecosystem when backed by the world's No. 1 exchange Binance and ecosystem such as Trust wallet, Pancake, Julswap, DODO, ... All of that makes BSC becomes one of the most popular leading blockchains in the DeFi industry. That is why we chose Binance Smart Chain.

BSCS Swap

BSC Swap is a decentralized exchange for swapping BEP-20 tokens on Binance Smart Chain with many other features that let user earn token and profit. BSC Swap uses an automated market maker (AMM) model where users trade against a liquidity pool.

They can use those tokens to reclaim their share, plus a portion of the trading fees.It's faster, cheaper and anyone can use it easily even beginner

The liquidity provided to the exchange comes from Liquidity Providers ("LPs") who stake their tokens in "Pools". When user make a token swap (trade) on the exchange user will pay a 0.2% trading fee, which is broken down as follows:

0.17% - Returned to liquidity pools in the form of a trading fee reward for liquidity providers.

0.03% - Sent to the BSC Swap Treasury to buy back BSCS and burn.

Launch APP

Liquidity Mining

To encourage participation in daily token groups, we are launching a liquidity mining program for users using BSC Swap.

In the way of great DeFi projects like Pancake Swap, Burger Swap ..., this program will distribute a fixed amount of BSCS tokens on a daily basis. All liquidity providers will receive BSCS from this unspecified fixed amount on a prorated basis. Unclaimed tokens will be sent back to the reward pool for later distribution.

AMM

Automated Market Makers allow digital assets to be traded without permission and automatically by using liquidity pools instead of a traditional market of buyers and sellers. AMMs have a different approach to trading assets.

AMMs are a financial tool unique to BSC, Ethereum and DeFi. This new technology is decentralized, always available for trading, and does not rely on the traditional interaction between buyers and sellers. This new method of exchanging assets embodies the general ideals of Ethereum and crypto in general: no one entity controls, everyone can participate, and anyone can build on top of another.

Startpool

BSC Startpool helps quality NFT and non-NFT projects distribute their tokens to a wider audience while allowing crypto users and investors to generate attractive returns in a form of new tokens. This is an innovative solution that gives advantages to both users and quality crypto projects.

Farmers can earn the new token for a total of 30 days. The first seven of those days are before the token is listed on BSC Swap, at which point farmers can immediately trade any tokens you have earned.

The tokens farmer earn are distributed to you daily, and you can add or remove tokens you’ve subscribed to the eligible pools at any time.

BSCS Dex

BSCS Dex is a decentralized exchange built on top of Binance Smart Chain. BSCS Dex will implement the AMM protocol and may in the future be replaced by PMM to get the best liquidity of the Binance Smart Chain to facilitate ordering on its chain. BSCS Dex will have the full functionality of a centralized exchange but furthermore it will help users to trade actively and conveniently.

Wallet

BSCS Wallet is the official crypto currency wallet of BSC Station included: mobile wallet and extension wallet on browser. BSCS Wallet allows users to safely and conveniently store many major cryptocurrencies in the market. In addition, the platform also directly integrates many DApp applications, Art & NFT collection to help users get closer to blockchain technology.
